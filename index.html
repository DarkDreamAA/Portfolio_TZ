Welcome to my Portfolio

Engineer Student - Thomas Zennaro - Innovative Smart Systems (ISS)

Promotion 54 - INSA Toulouse - 2020/2021

Abstract


The main objective of this portfolio gathers is to highlight the different skills that I developed during my last year at INSA Toulouse, from September 2020 to January 2021. I decided to follow the Innovative Smart Systems programme (ISS). ISS is a multidisciplinary programme because, students from different fields like electronic field, computer field, physical filed and also management field are gathered in order to bring several backgrounds useful for the different projects proposed during the semester. Thanks to the ISS programme, I am able to innovate, that is to say able to offer, design, produce, distribute and market a "smart system "starting from the component to the business application taking into account the societal aspects. Subsequently, this Portfolio explains my realized work and the skills that I acquired. Each course is divided into three themes: a descriptive part, an analytical part and a technical part. You can therefore choose to consult specific points of a course but also the entire course thanks to the main menu. Finally, a part "Generalities" includes my personal information, my Curriculum Vitae and the course modules.
